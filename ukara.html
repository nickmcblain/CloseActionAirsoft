UKARA

Do you want to wield your own weapon in the field?

WE are a UKARA registered site.

This page will link you to the information you need in order to become UKARA registered as a skirmisher.

You need to be UKARA registered ( and 18 or over) in order to purchase your own RIF (replica immitation firearm).

You need to attend 3 skirmishes in NOT LESS than 2 months in order to become registered.

We will stamp your UKARA form on or after your third skirmish, then YOU send it off or take it to a retailer. They will then enter your details on the UKARA database.
