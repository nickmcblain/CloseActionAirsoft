About us

At Close Action we pride ourselves on providing a service where experienced and novice players alike will have an unforgettable day. Groups, teams and individuals are all welcome and will be made to feel at home by our highly trained staff. Our skirmish days provide a variety of experiences that range from short, sharp, action-packed games, to longer, themed scenarios where players can use tactics and fieldcraft to achieve set objectives. We have a large team of player-marshalls who ensure that our reputation for fair play and fun gaming is upheld.

Game Times

Play starts at 1000 hours, you will get kitted up and ready for your safety briefing before jumping into the game zone, we allow for food at lunchtime to be ordered around 1245 hours, the day ends at 1630 hours.
